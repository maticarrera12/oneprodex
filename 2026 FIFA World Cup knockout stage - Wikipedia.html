From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The knockout stage of the 2026 FIFA World Cup will be the second and final stage of the competition, following the group stage. Played from June 28 to July 19, 2026, the knockout stage will end with the final, to be held at MetLife Stadium in East Rutherford, New Jersey.[1] The top two teams from each of the twelve groups, along with the best eight third-placed teams, will advance to the knockout stage to compete in a single-elimination tournament. There will be 32 matches in the knockout stage, including a match for third place.[2]

Format

[edit]

The knockout stage of the 2026 FIFA World Cup will be contested between 32 teams that qualify from the group stage. Matches in the knockout stage will be played to a finish. If the score of a match is level at the end of 90 minutes of playing time, 30 minutes of extra time will be played. If the scores were still tied, the match will be decided by a penalty shootout.[2] All times listed are local.[1]

In the round of 32, four of the group winners play group runners up. The other eight group winners play advancing 3rd-placed teams. The remaining group runners up play other group runners up.

FIFA set out the following schedule for the round of 32:[2]

  • Match 73: Runner-up Group A vs Runner-up Group B
  • Match 74: Winner Group E vs Best 3rd place Group A/B/C/D/F
  • Match 75: Winner Group F vs Runner-up Group C
  • Match 76: Winner Group C vs Runner-up Group F
  • Match 77: Winner Group I vs Best 3rd place Group C/D/F/G/H
  • Match 78: Runner-up Group E vs Runner-up Group I
  • Match 79: Winner Group A vs Best 3rd place Group C/E/F/H/I
  • Match 80: Winner Group L vs Best 3rd place Group E/H/I/J/K
  • Match 81: Winner Group D vs Best 3rd place Group B/E/F/I/J
  • Match 82: Winner Group G vs Best 3rd place Group A/E/H/I/J
  • Match 83: Runner-up Group K vs Runner-up Group L
  • Match 84: Winner Group H vs Runner-up Group J
  • Match 85: Winner Group B vs Best 3rd place Group E/F/G/I/J
  • Match 86: Winner Group J vs Runner-up Group H
  • Match 87: Winner Group K vs Best 3rd place Group D/E/I/J/L
  • Match 88: Runner-up Group D vs Runner-up Group G
